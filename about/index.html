About Josh Thiele

I value constant professional and personal growth as I continue my education at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and pursue a career in analytics. These objectives encourage me to challenge myself daily inside and outside the classroom.

I am majoring in Actuarial Science with minors in Mathematics, Statistics, and Business Analytics. I am a student in the 6th cohort of the Nebraska Business Honors Academy; this organization focuses on an academically rigorous curriculum, leadership, and community involvement.

Besides my formal education, I volunteer within the Huskers Athletics Sports Analytics department. This opportunity allows me to collect and analyze performance data to improve the performance of the University’s teams. Additionally, I am a research assistant within the College of Business, working on a project analyzing how NCAA football coaching turnover affects player performance. My desire to improve a team’s performance stems from my own passion and enjoyment of sports, which combines well with my analytical skills.
